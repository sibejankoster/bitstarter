FAQ

How does R(e)bot work?
R(e)bot records sound snippets of 10 seconds or longer, using your smart phone. The recording set includes better microphones that can be worn unobtrusively. By using these microphones, recording can be fully automatic, and requires no special attention. Without the recording set, you can still record unobtrusively. Taking out your smartphone will automatically start the recording of an audio snippet. No need to push buttons or even look at the device. The R(e)bot app will let you select and edit snippets, or save them all. You can also import photos and create a sonic album.
What is the battery life ?
With snippet length set to 10 seconds and frequency to once every 15 minutes, batteries in the recording set will last 50 hours continuously. The battery life of your smart device will not be effected much by the app.
What is the recording quality ?
This depends on the location of the microphones, whether you use the recording set, and the noise level at the location. Sound quality will be best using the external microphones from the set.
What is included in the set ?
The set includes of the app for Apple devices or Android, bluetooth transmitter, left and right microphones, heart rate sensor and skin resistance sensor, battery charger and the manual. The manual also contains instructions how to make a sonic diary with photographs and how to share these with friends.
