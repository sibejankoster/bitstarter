Sounds influence your emotions more directly than images, and are more
effective in bringing back memories and sharing emotions with friends and relatives.

Most probably, the moments that you experience new emotions will not
be the same moments that you take out your camera for taking a photograph.

The Rhythm Of Life app uses the connectivity of your smart device
to give you a much better log of your epic moments.
Depending on the accessories you have, you can control the recording
of soundscapes manually from your smart device, record at regular intervals
or start the recording based on your hart rate, movements or skin resistanc
using accessories such as hidden microphones, heart rate sensors and skin sensors.
You can share your recordings with your friends by uploading files to facebook.
