Do you want to make your day memorable, or do you need to remember what you did every hour of the day ?
AudioLog will help you remember and bring back the rhythm of your life
. On a holiday, you can can make a sonic diary or write a conventional one,
on a working day you can accurately fill out your project administration.

DayLog is an app for your smart device that logs your day by recording short audio fragments at regular intervals. You can play back your day in the time it takes to butter a sandwich, or listen to recorded soundscapes and share your emotions with friends.
AudioLog in automatic mode is easy to set up, and does not need your further attention.
Audiolog can also be triggered by your heartbeat or skin resistance (sensors required) to record your emotional or epic moments.
This goes way further than your camera, that you typically don't use at these moments.

For best quality, use an external button microphone that you can clip on your
clothing while on a holiday or visiting an art performance (available at the store).
